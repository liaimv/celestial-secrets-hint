Southern Star Constellations

Southern star constellations are patterns of stars visible from the southern hemisphere. These main constellations have been identified and named for their shapes, making it easier to locate and study stars in the southern sky.

Zodiac Sign

The zodiac is a set of twelve constellations along the Sun's apparent path in the sky. Each zodiac sign has a symbol, an associated element, and a representative constellation.

Northern Star Constellations

Northern star constellations are patterns of stars visible from the northern hemisphere. On this page, you can explore not only the main constellations but also the Greek alphabet labeling system. Stars are labeled with Greek letters, with Alpha for the brightest star, Beta for the next brightest, and so on. This system helps classify stars by brightness within each constellation. Note that some stars do not have a Greek letter designation.
